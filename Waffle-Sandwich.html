Waffle Sandwich

In order to make a waffle sandiwch you first need

  1. Three store bought waffles.
  2. Peanut butter
  3. Jelly
  4. Toaster
  5. Freezer

First you need to put your three store bought waffles in the freezer. Then after a day you can take out your three store bought waffles and put them both in a toaster. You want to toast it at a light setting for a minute. After you take your waffles out. Then you want to take out your peanut butter and jelly and spread the panut butter on one of the waffles and your jelly on another waffle. Then put it back in toaster and set it at medium. Then you take out your waffles and stack them. Finally, you eat your newly made waffle sandwich.
